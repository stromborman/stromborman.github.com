Strom Borman

Research scientist at Yahoo working at the intersection of machine learning and control theory.

Previously was a mathematician doing research in symplectic geometry and applied topology. Assistant Professor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Post-Docs at Stanford University and Institute for Advanced Study, and Ph.D. from University of Chicago.
